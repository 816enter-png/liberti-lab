About the Studio

Where material craft meets digital intelligence.

Liberti Lab is an industrial design studio and maker space in Jersey City, NJ. We prototype physical products, build wearable hardware systems, and create sculptural objects from recycled plastics.

Our work spans consumer electronics, medical devices, and limited-edition art objects — united by a belief that the best design is built by hand before it's refined by machine.
